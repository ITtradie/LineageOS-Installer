Prepare your device

Set up your device:

  1. Go to About Phone (or Tablet).
  2. Tap "Build Number" 7 times.
  3. Go back to Setting's home
  4. Tap "Developer Settings"
  5. Enable "USB Debugging."
  6. Enable "OEM Unlocking." This option may not be present on all devices; if so, your manufacturer should have enabled this option by default
